Sleep Tracker

This program calculates the user's average sleep in a week.

Features
1. Calculates total sleep (in hours) in a week
2. Displays average sleep in hours and minutes
3. Provides assessment based on the average sleep
4. Handles Fractional cases 
   example: 5.71 hours as 5 hours 42 minutes
5. Does not execute for unrealistic sleep values

SAMPLE OUTPUT 1
52.50 is the total hours in the week
Your Average Sleep is 7 hours and 30 minutes per week
Your average sleep is healthy! Keep it up.

SAMPLE OUTPUT 2
48.50 is the total hours in the week
Your Average Sleep is 6 hours and 55 minutes per week
Your average sleep is decent but a little more could be used.
